  Comprehensive redesign of training infrastructure.

  hypotheses, so all three are fixed in one version to establish a correctly
  formulated base model.

  BCE + card_id%32 hash + ATTACK/END hard veto compound to prevent meaningful
  evaluation of incremental changes. A system with per-decision CE, card metadata
  features, and extended confidence-gated overrides will (a) train more efficiently

  The holdout hybrid_top1 should match or exceed v0-06d11 (0.4507), and ATTACK coverage
  should be measurable rather than suppressed.
